## üìù Presentation Tips

### Timing Breakdown:
- **Slide 1 (Problem):** 20 seconds
- **Slide 2 (Solution):** 30 seconds
- **Slide 3 (Demo):** 40 seconds
- **Slide 4 (Impact):** 20 seconds
- **Slide 5 (Vision):** 10 seconds
- **Total:** 2 minutes

### Delivery Notes:
1. **Energy:** Start with high energy on the problem - make them feel the pain
2. **Pace:** Slow down for the demo - let the product speak
3. **Confidence:** End with conviction - you're solving a real problem
4. **Eye Contact:** Look at the audience, not the slides
5. **Pause:** After "BAM!" give them a second to see the magic

### Pro Tips:
- Have the demo pre-loaded and ready to go
- Practice the transition to demo smoothly
- If demo fails, have screenshots as backup
- End with a clear call to action
- Smile when you say "QuantCoach LiveKit"

### Slide Layouts:

**Slide 1:** Problem
- Large impactful statistic: "73%" in huge text
- Sub-bullets with pain points
- Image of stressed person with too many screens

**Slide 2:** Solution
- Logo prominently displayed
- Clean product screenshot
- 4-5 bullet points with icons

**Slide 3:** Demo
- Full screen of live demo or video
- Minimal text overlays showing key features
- Arrow annotations pointing to important UI elements

**Slide 4:** Impact
- Three large numbers with up arrows
- Simple icons for each metric
- Minimal text, maximum impact

**Slide 5:** Vision
- Clean, aspirational image
- Logo again
- Tagline prominently displayed
